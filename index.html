For Guides

You've been where they are.
That makes you qualified.

You don't need a certification, a fast time, or a coaching degree. You just need to have been through the beginner phase yourself — and care enough to help someone else through it.

Here's how it works for you:

1
Sign up in 30 seconds

Tell us about your running background. No test. No resume. Just your experience and willingness to help.

2
Get your dashboard + tools

We give you ready-made training plans, weekly check-in prompts, and milestone templates. You don't have to build anything from scratch.

3
Get matched with a beginner

We pair you with someone who's ready to start. You choose: guide someone in your neighborhood, or connect with someone anywhere in the country.

4
Guide them to their first mile

Check in weekly. Celebrate the small wins. Use our tools or make it your own. Watch someone become a runner because you believed in them.

What you need to know:

  • Your first runner is completely free — no cost, no catch
  • Want to guide more people? It's $2/month per additional runner
  • You need about 10 minutes a week to check in
  • Guide someone locally (run together, meet up) or remotely (text, message, virtual)
  • No speed requirement — a 12-minute miler is just as qualified as a 7-minute miler
  • You've been running 6+ months consistently — that's the only bar

"I'm not a coach. I'm just a runner who remembers how hard it was to start — and I want to make it easier for someone else."

For New Runners

You don't need to figure this out alone.

Starting to run is hard. Not because your body can't do it — but because nobody's there to tell you it's normal to feel like this. We fix that. We connect you with a real runner who's been exactly where you are.

Here's how it works for you:

1
Tell us where you're at

Never run before? Tried and quit? Ran in high school and haven't since? All good. Just tell us your starting point.

2
Get matched with a guide

We pair you with an experienced runner who gets it. Someone who's been through the hard part and wants to help you through yours. Local or remote — your choice.

3
Follow a simple plan together

Your guide gives you a week-by-week plan. No guesswork. No overwhelming training schedules. Just "here's what to do this week."

4
Run your first mile

With someone checking in, celebrating your progress, and telling you "you can do this" — you're going to get there. And you'll never forget the day you do.

What you need to know:

  • It's free — your guide is volunteering their time to help you
  • This is 1-on-1 — not a group, not a class, just you and your guide
  • Connect with someone in your area to run together, or find a guide anywhere who checks in virtually
  • No pressure, no judgment — your pace is the right pace
  • You'll get a simple plan, weekly encouragement, and someone who actually cares if you show up

"I just needed someone to tell me it was okay to walk, that it gets easier, and that I wasn't too slow. That's it. That's all I needed to keep going."

Why we're building this

There's nothing like this. Pro coaching platforms charge hundreds a month and require certifications. AI apps have no human connection. Running buddy apps match people to run together — but not to guide someone who hasn't started yet.

We're runners who believe the best thing about this sport isn't the medals — it's the people who helped us along the way. So we're building a place where that help is structured, simple, and free.

Grassroots. Human. Real.
